I started in graphic design and drifted into video editing the moment a client asked for "just a quick animated version" of a poster. Six years later that's most of what I do.

I work across brand films, title sequences, and short-form social content — usually starting in Illustrator or Figma for the visual system, then carrying it into Premiere and After Effects for motion.

Based between two time zones, always up for a project that needs both a good cut and a strong visual language.
